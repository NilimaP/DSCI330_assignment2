Course Description

A thorough study of the chemistry of organic compounds, with emphasis on structure, properties, synthesis, purification, principles and mechanisms of reactions, instrumental methods, compound identification, and important biological and economic applications. Prerequisites: For CHEM 350: CHEM 212 and CHEM 213; for CHEM 351: CHEM 350 or CHEM 340 or CHEM 341 with instructor's permission.
